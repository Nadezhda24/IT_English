Definition

Database adminitrator are the professionals who help store, secure and organize that data.

Analogue of in Russian: Администратор баз данных

Database adminitrators are tasked with building databases that logically store information in a way that works well with the systems that may access this information. They may also be responsible for data recovery efforts and creating data backups.

Typical duties:

  • Creating back-ups of data and recovering lost data
  • Merging existing databases into new databases
  • Ensuring user permissions for data use are valid and up to date
  • Maintaining proper function of all databases
